Rewards & Risks

Rewards

World's #2 robotaxi operator — Apollo Go: 3.2M fully driverless rides in Q1-26 (+120% YoY), weekly peak >350k, 27 cities incl. Dubai, ~70% of Waymo's weekly volume; Wuhan reached unit-cost breakeven
AI Cloud Infra +79% YoY (GPU cloud +184%) — now bigger than the ad business for the first time; Kunlun chips (M100/M300) + >RMB1B China Mobile order signal real monetization
Balance sheet is the floor — cash + investments $40.5B > the entire $36.5B market cap; ~$83/ADS broad net value vs $107.24 spot
Cheapest liquid way to own the robotaxi theme — 14.4x forward earnings vs Tesla's 150x+ for a still-pilot-scale operation
Thai DR (BIDU80) references the HK line (9888.HK), sidestepping direct US ADR-delisting risk; liquid 2-20M units/day

Risks

Core ad business is melting FASTER, not stabilizing — -7% (Q4-25) → -22% (Q1-26); AI chatbots cannibalize search traffic and ad load, and nobody knows where the floor is
Profit compression while transitioning — Q1-26 GAAP net income RMB3.4B (-55% YoY), FCF negative; sell-side kept cutting targets (BofA $180→$165 Jul-14, Barclays $128→$124 Jul-10) heading into the Q2 print (~Aug 2026)
AI Cloud price war — Alibaba/Tencent/ByteDance/Huawei all discount aggressively; the +79% growth rate could decelerate hard
Robotaxi optionality may never monetize for shareholders — locked out of the US (largest profit pool), low China ride prices, local-license politics, a future Apollo spin-off could strand value
VIE/ADR structure + persistent China discount — mitigated for the DR holder (BIDU80 references the HK line) but the valuation discount on China assets is a market regime, not a temporary mispricing
