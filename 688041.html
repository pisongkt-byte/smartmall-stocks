DCU still trails Nvidia on raw performance / software maturity; the x86 gap vs Intel/AMD widens while the Zen IP stays frozen.
Thin FCF (~¥0.9B) + ¥6.4B inventory build = cash-hungry growth; STAR-market lock-ups, insider selling & index flows amplify swings. HYGON80 DR is new & illiquid.
