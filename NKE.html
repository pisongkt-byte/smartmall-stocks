Rewards

Risks

Greater China −17% cc in Q4, 6 straight quarters of decline and getting worse — this is the key un-fixed piece of the turnaround, ~13% of sales but historically the highest-margin market
Value-trap risk: core EPS ex-refund is only ~$1.58 (not reported $2.10) → true P/E is ~28x, not the headline 21x — if core earnings don't recover toward $2.60-3.00 by FY28, today's price isn't actually cheap
Dividend payout ~104% of core EPS — buybacks already sacrificed to protect it; a second down year would raise real questions about the 24-year streak
Converse −31% FY26 and digital −12% — parts of the 'fix' still not working, some of digital decline may be real demand loss, not just intentional promo pullback
On (+25%) and Hoka continue taking premium-running share, and Adidas is riding a retro-lifestyle wave Nike missed — competitors keep compounding while Nike repairs itself
