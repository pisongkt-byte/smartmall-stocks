Risks

China export-control cliff — US/Dutch rules cap what ASML can ship to China (~20% of 2026 sales); a tightening could pull a chunk of DUV demand overnight
Valuation — stock ~3x off its low to a near-$2,000 ATH at ~56x trailing / ~40x FY26e earnings; priced for perfection, any demand wobble de-rates hard
Cyclicality — semi-equipment is boom-bust; a hyperscaler capex digestion or memory downturn hits orders, and ASML stopped disclosing quarterly bookings in 2026, reducing forward visibility
Customer concentration + High-NA adoption risk — a handful of buyers (TSMC, Samsung, Intel); Intel's foundry struggles and any single customer's capex cut swing orders, and High-NA volume economics are still unproven
