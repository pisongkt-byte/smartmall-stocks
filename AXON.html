Rewards

~50% below the 52-week high of $886 — most de-rating since the run-up has already happened

Risks

Valuation is the whole debate: ~13x trailing / ~10x fwd sales, ~42x fwd P/E. Any slip toward 20% growth re-rates it down hard
SBC = $634M (23% of revenue) crushed GAAP earnings (NI $377M->$125M, op income negative) and dilutes ~4-5%/yr (76.6M->80.6M shares in a year, verified) — a material per-share drag
Customer base = US state & local police budgets — politically sensitive (defund cycles, municipal austerity)
Regulatory / civil-liberties headlines: TASER use-of-force litigation, body-cam privacy, AI report-writing accuracy/bias
Key-man risk: founder-CEO Rick Smith; M&A integration + ~$1B/quarter cash burn on acquisitions
