Rewards & Risks

Rewards

Trades at a discount to through-cycle earnings — ~20x normalized 2026 EPS (~$7.50) and ~9x management's 2029 adj-EPS target (~$25); 2026 is called the trough
#2 pure-play Medicaid MCO — ~$32B Medicaid premium, ~5.5M members; still positive Medicaid pretax margin even at the cycle bottom
Embedded 'new-store' earnings >$11/share from won-but-immature contracts (Illinois, Florida) → 2027 premium-revenue target ~$48B
Two big 2025-26 drags reverse in 2027: Medicaid rate restoration + exit of the loss-making MAPD Medicare Part D product; CMS already lifted 2027 Medicare rates
Aggressive buybacks shrinking the share count (57.7M→52.1M) at depressed prices; investment-grade balance sheet, net cash ~$4.6B

Risks

Medical Cost Ratio / margin volatility — consolidated MCR jumped to 91.7% (from 89.1%); the 'rate-vs-trend imbalance' IS the story and could persist past the '2026 trough' call, pinning net margin near 1%
Rate-setting & political risk — revenue is set by governments (state Medicaid rates, CMS Medicare, ACA subsidies); ACA enhanced-subsidy expiry and Medicaid funding cuts could shrink both membership and margin
Redetermination / membership attrition — post-COVID Medicaid redeterminations already cut membership (4.89M→4.57M); further disenrollment or adverse acuity pressures premium and MCR
Contract / RFP risk — growth hinges on winning state re-procurements; a single lost contract can erase a multi-billion revenue block overnight
The recovery is a forecast, not a fact — 'trough,' >$11 embedded EPS and the $25 2029 target are management's; at ~44x FY26 earnings the stock has no cushion if medical-cost trend keeps beating rates
