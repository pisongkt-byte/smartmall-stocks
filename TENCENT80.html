Games cash engine at record scale — international games +33% FY25, crossed US$10B for the first time (Supercell, PUBG Mobile, VALORANT, Wuthering Waves); domestic +18% with Delta Force now 'evergreen'
Ads re-rating on AI — Marketing Services +19% FY25 / +20% Q1-26 with Weixin ad-load still far below peers; AI ad-targeting (AIM+) ~30% of spend, closed-loop commerce via Mini Programs + Video Accounts
Pristine balance sheet + shareholder returns — net cash +RMB147B, record FCF RMB182.6B (+18%), HK$80B/yr buyback shrinking the share count + rising dividend
Cheap for the quality — ~12x forward, and only ~10-11x on the core once net cash + the RMB912B investment book are stripped; SOTP fair value ~HK$600 (฿25.9) ≈ +36% base upside

Risks
